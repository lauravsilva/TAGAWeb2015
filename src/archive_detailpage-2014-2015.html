RIT TAGA Publication 2014 - 2015

About This Publication

The distinguishing visual factor for our publication is a “Swiss” design aesthetic. We wanted to create a simplified design that is not only easy to follow but visually appealing for the reader. The RIT Chapter takes pride in the hard work put forth to bring a modern look and feel to our publication through ideas first brought about in the 1940s in Switzerland. The printed book is bound with a matte coated soft cover. There is variable data included in our book, creating personalized editions for each respective school or judge attending the conference as well as editions for current RIT TAGA members and the individuals in industry and at RIT who have helped make this publication possible. Included with this publication is a mix of old and new technology, a bookmark printed with a digital printer as well as a Vandercook Universal I Letterpress using a 3D printed block. To complement our print publication, we created a sleek and visually pleasing digital publication using the Adobe Digital Publishing Suite.
