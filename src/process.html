The Process

Fundraising

Raise money for TAGA through various events, including bake sales, t-shirt sales, and other public events. Collaborate with the Budget Team to ascertain TAGA funds.

Copy Editing

Curate suitable papers about printing and publishing. Perform editing to ensure they are professional and grammatically correct.

Creative & Design

Manage the look and functionality of our book and ensure visual consistency across platforms. Ideate innovative solutions to make our book unique.

Photo & Video

Create photos and videos of our process to be used for our website, app, and fundraising content.

Website

Design our website to be user-friendly and carry our current theme into an online platform. Ensure proper mobile and tablet optimization.

Digital

Create a digital publication that complements the printed edition using Adobe Digital Publishing Suite. Create a digital book for iPad and iPhone, utilizing proper UI/UX design.

Print

Print a high quality book using various printing methods such as flexography, digital printing, lithography, and 3D printing. Ensure quality and cost effectiveness throughout the entire process.
